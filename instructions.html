01

Overview

An 8x8 - 2 player turn-based board game consisting of 5 different pieces.

  • Titan
  • Tank
  • Cannon
  • Ricochet
  • Semi Ricochet


Any piece can move one tile or rotate once. The cannon is allowed to move only in the horizontal direction and is positioned in the base rank.

The aim of the game is to destroy the opponent’s Titan by striking the bullet through a series of various Ricochets.

02

Pieces

Titan: Heart and soul of the lineup. If it is taken down, you go down as well.

Cannon: A piece equipped with the technology to take the Titan down with its bullet shot after it's team's turn. It can maximum take 3 shots

Tank: Piece with heafty health which can block the opponent’s bullet. Its initial Health is 5 shots.

Ricochet: Tactical piece that can deflect the path of any bullet that hits it.

Semi ricochet: Lazier version of the ricochet that allows delfection only when bullet travels in a certian path.

03

Specifics

Each player gets 300 seconds. The timer decrements during the respective players turn. If the timer balls down to 0, the other player wins.

Cannon can move only in the horizontal direction.

After a player does his move, his cannon launches a bullet, so keep your titan safe!

All The remaining pieces can move one square distance anywhere on the board, either diagonally,horizontally or vertically.

Ricochets and semi-ricochets can rotate on the same square, changing their surface of deflection.

Semi-ricochets can be destroyed if hit on their non-reflecting surface.

Ricochets can swap with any piece but the Titan on the board.

Tanks allow their respective bullet the pass through.

Use spells wisely to gain tactical advantage.

04

Spells

Heal: Adds 1 bar of health to the ally Tank.

Strike: Reduces 1 bar of health to the opponent's Tank.

Block: Blocks an empty square for the entire duration of the match.

Vanish: Titan will not be affected by bullets as long as it is in the particular square .
